## Design Context

### Users
German business buyers — regional founders, service businesses, and broader clients who are evaluating website partners. They browse on desktop and mobile, often during business hours. They're skeptical of generic agencies and respond immediately to visible quality, confident positioning, and real proof of work. They need to understand the offer within seconds and feel that Metz & Partner operates at a genuinely higher level than anything they've seen before.

### Brand Personality
**Three words:** cinematic, precise, confident

Tone: a design-led studio with sharp judgment and restrained confidence. Not luxury for its own sake — expensive *because* it's controlled, intentional, and honest. The site itself is the proof. No fake maturity signals. No agency clichés.

Emotional goal: visitors should feel a combination of "I haven't seen anything like this from a local agency" and "I understand exactly what they do and want to call them."

### Aesthetic Direction
**Dark glass system.** Two-layer architecture that must hold throughout the site:

- **Atmosphere (Layer 1)**: Living animated background. Currently: `DotFieldBackground` (dot grid, near-black). The warm amber/copper atmosphere from DESIGN_SYSTEM.md has been intentionally superseded — the current direction pairs the dark field with a lime accent `#D3FD51`. This creates a more electric, precise mood vs. the earlier warm-organic one.
- **Glass (Layer 2)**: All content lives in luminous glass panels over the atmosphere. `backdrop-filter: blur(20–40px)`, luminous top-edge highlight (1px white), faint ambient glow, near-transparent base. **Glass is the primary organizational language.** No flat matte cards.

**Accent**: Lime `#D3FD51` — used sparingly (10% rule). CTAs, active dots, hover states only. Not a decorative fill.

**Reference points**: ReactBits, Resend, Unseen.co, Oryzo.ai

**Anti-references**: Generic SaaS startup sites, shadcn default UI, flat matte cards, icon-grid feature sections, cyan/purple AI gradient palettes, warm amber-dominant direction (superseded).

**Theme**: Dark. Business buyers evaluating a significant purchase — critical, often office setting, where dark premium signals "this is serious."

### Typography
**Display: `Instrument_Serif`** — user preference, keep as-is. Italic treatment is a core visual signature of the site.

**UI: `DM_Sans`** — user preference. Clean, Helvetica/SF Pro–adjacent feel at body sizes. Keep as-is.

### WhyUs Section Recommendation
**Keep, but repositioned.** The current WhyUs (`#ueber-uns`) is actually doing two important jobs:
1. Founder cards → personal/direct trust signal ("you talk to us, not an account manager")
2. Differentiators → 3 concrete reasons to choose M&P

**Problem**: Two-part structure with equal visual weight feels like two separate sections crammed together. The headline "Wenn Sie uns anfragen, sprechen Sie mit uns." is strong copy — the section just doesn't commit to it fully.

**Recommendation**: Elevate the headline as the section's single strong claim. Make the founder cards the primary visual — asymmetric layout (not a 50/50 grid). The differentiators should feel like supporting details underneath, not a separate card grid. The section should feel like a confident introduction, not a features list.

**Do not remove.** The founders are a genuine differentiator for a small agency. Personal presence is real proof.

### Proof / Projects Recommendation
Currently: one client project with only a Hero screenshot. 

**Recommendation**: Add a before/after treatment. Frame both in browser glass mockups side-by-side or stacked. Even one real before/after is stronger than any amount of explanatory copy. Keep the scope honest — one real project presented with precision is premium; three weak screenshots are not.

If a second client screenshot is available (full page or additional sections), present it as a multi-frame sequence rather than a single screenshot.

### Design Principles
1. **Glass is the organizational language** — every meaningful content surface is a glass panel over the dot-field atmosphere. Never flat cards, never matte panels.
2. **Proof over explanation** — show real work in browser-frame glass mockups. One real before/after > three paragraphs.
3. **One offer, total clarity** — services section presents one focused thing (`Webdesign & Entwicklung`) with internal module logic. Never a multi-service grid.
4. **Motion is cinematic, not scattered** — each section earns one strong animation moment. Framer Motion for entrances/hover, GSAP for scroll-driven sequences.
5. **Honest ambition** — no fake testimonials, no inflated metrics, no invented trust signals. The design quality itself is the primary trust signal.
6. **Personal presence matters** — founders are a real differentiator. Keep them visible and human, not corporate.
